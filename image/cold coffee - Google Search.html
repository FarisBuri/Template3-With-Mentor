      Iced coffee

      Description

      Description

      Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee in the normal way and then serving it over ice or in cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold. In hot brewing, sweeteners and flavoring may be added before cooling, as they dissolve faster. Wikipedia
      Main ingredient: Coffee, Ice
      Serving temperature: Cold
      Variation: Iced mocha, Iced latte
